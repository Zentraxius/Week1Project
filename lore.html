Modern Changes

Many things are different from what you know. There are changes going on in the world that they don't want you to know about. To begin, we will cover what happened from the viewpoint in time of 2032, the year Sam first attended The School.

Formerly, the vampires of the world used to do their best to hide in plain site, and simply blend in. Those times have past and they have made their move on a global scale. It started by allying themselves with the United Nations a few years past. It has long been known amongst the most average of first world citizens, that the major world leaders no longer cared for them, and those who did were kept from power by any means necessary. The Head's of House used this to their advantage to begin making their move. By connecting with the few uncorrupt world leaders and installing their own slowly where necessary, they began to align themselves with powerful allies on a global scale within the rooms of the UN. Rarely if ever meeting in person, their communications done in private, quickly becoming a seperate faction while still flying under the UN flag.

Armed with the power of close to 10% of the global economy, and formed by the guidance of the Headmaster, this split faction of the UN began to take action, and moved with an overwhelming confidence the UN had always meant to have. By simply bypassing the entirety of the political red tape and chest puffing the UN had become known for, and armed with more money and connections than any one man could dream of, they quickly established a UN Embassy in northern New York under the guise of making a global university for the worlds brightest and most talented. The entrance requirements quickly became headline news around the world for one simple fact. The school publicly welcomed vampire students and had roughly a third of its student body as such. While the embassy was founded in 2016, it had not become a school and operational until 2024.

Though the details of their relationship remains a mystery, the new Governor of New York who had taken office in 2022 was very welcoming and supportive of the school and its efforts and held the school in great confidence. It was because of this support that the many riots, attempted vandalism, and outrage, were quelled at the behest of the National Guard. Vampires had been, until now, fairly disliked worldwide, with the loose exception of Italy. Now, suddenly public, and tucked away safe within the confines of a UN Embassy, figureheads around the world were baffled, as was the main faction of the UN, especially when they discovered they no longer had a voice with this new faction that operated under their name.

This led to a worldwide cold war from the years of 2024 to 2031
