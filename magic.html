The Nephilem's Return

Magic and Mana

Magic functions in a strange manner, there is a finite amount of mana in the world, however all of it is recycled and returned to circulation eventually, it is not possible to erase magic, but it is possible to store mana in places that become lost.

Those learning to harness magic do so by learning of the different forms of Mana.

  1. Earth - The ground which we walk on and all within it
  2. Light - Given to us by the stars, and the absence thereof
  3. Kinetic - Manipulating the energy within all things that allows movement
  4. Force - The ability to produce kinetic energy from nothing, and keep it shapeless.
  5. Water - The liquid of life that sustains all creatures

Magic begins with only these elements. As your ability to hold more mana grows, you learn to combine elements to reach higher levels of precision and form. Eventually different types or sources of mana become available to you, such as Telekinesis, which uses your own mental energy, or life force, as fuel.

Natural Abilities

Ability is defined as a semi-magical or physically impossible capability that is not done through use of mana. Another name for it is simply Natural Magic. Vampires are the most noteworthy case of this
